Chrystian

Machine Learning TA at CS Dept., Gadjah Mada University

Hi! I am Chrystian. Currently, I am a final year undergraduate Computer Science student at Gadjah Mada University, Indonesia. Previously, I have worked here as a Lab Assistant, Research Assistant, and as of now, TA on two Machine Learning classes.

I work on and study deep generative models. My current goal is to apply GANs on real-world applications, but hopefully, somewhere near in the future, I also will be able to develop a new architecture that breaks the state-of-the-art. I am also interested in attention mechanisms such as Transformer and their application on many fields such as NLP, Classification, GANs.

For 2022 I am currently looking for any opportunities. Feel free to contact me anytime.
