Chrystian

AI Engineer at Samsung Research, Jakarta

Hi, my name is Chrystian, and I am currently an AI Engineer at Samsung Research Indonesia. Prior to this role, I also interned as an AI Engineer at Max Planck Institute for Biological Cybernetics in Tübingen, Germany after completing my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science at Gadjah Mada University.

I have always been curious about the inner workings of seemingly black-box machine learning models. During my early undergraduate years, I was intrigued and worked on computer vision, and later on unsupervised generative modeling. Later, as an intern at Max Planck Society, I delved into and experimented with self-supervised learning, the neuroscience of vision, and reinforcement learning.

As of now, my main interest and work are in machine learning database technology and neural network-based reinforcement learning models. In my free time, I am trying to gain a more in-depth understanding of deep learning and exploring ways to optimize AI to make it more usable and accessible to everyone.
